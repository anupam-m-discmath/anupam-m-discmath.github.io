About me

I'm a student of mathematics, interested in problems in combinatorics and discrete mathematics.

Areas of interest

  • Combinatorial topology: abstract simplicial complexes, simplicial homology, discrete Morse theory etc.
  • Combinatorial and discrete geometry
  • Enumerative and topological combinatorics
